CloudClearance Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 22, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how CloudClearance ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects the personal information of users ("you", "your", or "yours"). By using our Discord bot, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information Collected

We collect the following types of information when you interact with our bot:

  • Discord Account Data: username, user ID.
  • Command Information: specific requests made through the bot, such as weather queries.
  • Chat Messages: the bot analyzes messages to identify and respond to relevant commands.
  • API Data: weather data retrieved via the CheckWX API to provide accurate information.

2. Use of Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: to provide accurate weather information via the bot.
  • Service Improvement: to analyze interactions with the bot and improve its functionality.
  • Personalization: to tailor the bot's responses based on the history of requests.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal information based on the following legal grounds:

  • Performance of a Contract: to provide the services requested by the user.
  • Consent: where you have given consent for specific uses of your data.
  • Legitimate Interest: to improve our services and ensure their security.

4. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Obligation: when required by law or in response to a valid legal request.
  • Protection of Rights: to protect our rights, property, or safety, as well as those of our users.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of the information transmitted or stored.

6. Your Rights

In accordance with applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: you may request a copy of the information we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: you may request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: you may request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: you may request the restriction of processing your data in certain cases.
  • Right to Object: you may object to the processing of your data in specific situations.
  • Right to Data Portability: you may request the transfer of your data to another provider.

7. Limited Use

The CloudClearance bot is designed to provide information for simulation purposes only. It should not be used for real or critical decision-making, especially concerning safety, navigation, or aviation.
