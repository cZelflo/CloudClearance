CloudClearance Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 22, 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how CloudClearance collects, uses, and protects user information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our bot:

  • Basic Discord account information: username, user ID.
  • Command information: weather requests and airport inquiries made by users.
  • Chat messages: the bot reads messages sent in the chat to respond to commands.
  • API Data: weather data obtained via the CheckWX API and airport information via the AirportDB API.

2. Use of Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide accurate weather and airport information.
  • Improve and customize the features of our bot.
  • Analyze bot usage for future improvements.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or to protect our rights. Additionally, users must adhere to the terms of service of the CheckWX API and the AirportDB API when utilizing features provided by CloudClearance that involve data retrieval from these sources.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access and disclosure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information.

6. Limited Use

The bot is intended for simulation purposes only and should not be used for real-life or critical decisions.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at the following email address: cloudclearance.official@gmail.com.
